Hi! I am Dan.

I am a Product Designer who enjoys crafting delightful user experience across multiple platforms and devices. Currently I work at Babbel in Berlin, we aim to provide the best language learning experience.

With background in both industrial and interaction design, I am experienced in the entire design process: understanding users & metrics, ideation, prototyping, evaluation and iteration. Also, my teammates and colleagues often say they like to work with me:)

I am easily fascinated with any tiny pleasant details in life/ interesting people I've met / things I never tried before or any good combination of them. Besides designing stuff I'm busy with exploring new things, spotting cute faces, cooking or watching cartoons.
